I’m currently a PhD student at Stony Brook University, focusing on theoretical aspects of particle physics and cosmology. You can read more about me and my research on my academic webpage.

Prior to arriving at Stony Brook, I was an undergraduate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

I owe a lot of thanks to several other prolific bloggers, especially 4gravitons and Peter Woit (both of whom previously spent some time at Stony Brook, though we did not overlap), whose sites inspired me to start my own blog. I also received a lot of inspiration from Sabine Hossenfelder, Matt Strassler, and Tommaso Dorigo— if you like anything you’ve read on this site, you should definitely give their blogs a read, as they are the real masters of the craft.
